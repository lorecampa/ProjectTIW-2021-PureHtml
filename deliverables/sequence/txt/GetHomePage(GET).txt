title GetHomePage(GET)

participant GetHomePage
participant playlistDAO
participant AlbumDAO
participant ctx
participant TemplateEngine


GetHomePage ->playlistDAO: playlistDAO.findAllPlaylistByUserId(userId) 

GetHomePage <--playlistDAO: playlists

GetHomePage ->AlbumDAO: findAllUserAlbumsById(userId) 

GetHomePage <-- AlbumDAO: albums

GetHomePage ->ctx: setVariables(playlists)
GetHomePage ->ctx: setVariables(albums)

GetHomePage ->TemplateEngine: process(ctx, "HomePage.html")
